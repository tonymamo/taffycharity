Donate to the TAFFY General Fund

This fund is the foundation on which we are built. More often than not, our little grassroots operation finds someone in need, does an event, and gives all the cash to the recipient directly. This leaves our bank account empty and sometimes hinders our ability to help others. Having a General Fund gives T.A.F.F.Y. the ability to take action immediately and help a family in distress within hours instead of months.

All donations are securely processed through Paypal and go directly to the T.A.F.F.Y. General Fund. You do not need a Paypal account to donate, and you can donate with any major credit card or bank account.
